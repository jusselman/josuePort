JOSHUA USSELMAN

ABOUT

My name is Joshua Usselman. A Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, Photographer, and Epicurean who provides the most delicious tints, shades, and tones.


A native of Portland, Oregon, have shined in Palma de Mallorca, Guadalajara, and currently San Francisco. My objective is to make the whole world synesthetic by any means necessary.
